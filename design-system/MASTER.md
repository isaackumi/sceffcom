# SCEFFCOM Design System

## Visual Thesis
Midnight institute catalogue — deep navy authority on warm stone paper, antique gold accents, magazine-scale typography, photography as the visual anchor.

## Typography
- **Display:** Cormorant Garamond — headlines, pull quotes, program numbers
- **Body:** DM Sans — navigation, body copy, UI

## Colors
| Role | Hex | Token |
|------|-----|-------|
| Midnight | `#0B1628` | midnight |
| Stone | `#F7F4EE` | stone |
| Gold | `#B8860B` | gold |
| Slate | `#4A5D73` | slate |
| Ink | `#0C1222` | ink |

## Anti-patterns (avoid)
- Pill badge clusters, floating stat cards, grain overlays
- Fraunces/Inter/Playfair generic pairings
- Rounded-2xl card grids, terracotta AI palette
- Uppercase tracking eyebrow on every section

## Motion
- Staggered hero word reveal
- Subtle gold line draw on load
- Hover: color shift only, no scale layout shift
